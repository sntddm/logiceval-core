-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Using **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)** here is actually an exceptional engineering choice!

Instead of stuffing all 50+ or 100+ logical fallacies into a massive LLM context window every single time—which increases latency, blows up your token costs, and leads to "needle-in-a-haystack" confusion for the model—we can use RAG. When a user submits an argument, we can perform a quick semantic vector search against our `catalog` to pull only the top 3–5 most *logically relevant* fallacies and inject only those definitions into the AI prompt. This keeps your validation pipeline fast, accurate, and cheap.
